Company Overview

About Us

Khumo Holdings Group is a diversified, majority Black female‑owned and controlled business enterprise with strategic interests across multiple high‑impact sectors, including construction, technology, e‑commerce, real estate, mining, energy, and property asset management. The Group is deeply committed to empowerment, transformation, and inclusive economic growth, positioning itself as a leader in driving sustainable development across Southern Africa.
